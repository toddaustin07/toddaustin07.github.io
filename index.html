To get started with enabling your Raspberry Pi to run SmartThings direct connect device applications:

1) Download this file to your Pi's home directory: mastersetup

Right-click on link above, OR

From your Pi home directory:

pi@raspberrpi:~ $ wget http://toddaustin07.github.io/mastersetup

2) pi@raspberrypi:~ $ chmod +x mastersetup

3) pi@raspberrypi:~ $ ./mastersetup
